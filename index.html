The mysteries of the Bermuda Triangle

History

The Bermuda Triangle or otherwise known as the Devils triangel, is in a segmeant of the North Atlantic Ocean off North America. This area has a reputation for ships and airplanes mysteriously disappearing. Reports dating back as far as the mid-19th century, have shown a number of unexplained events in whichs ships had vanished. There are many conspiracy theories as to why this could have happened, the most common being a rouge wave or magnetic forces causing compasses to malfuntion.
